JOBS

Web Developer

Design, code, and maintain responsive websites. Collaborate with teams and clients to deliver innovative solutions.

Expectations:

  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks.
  • Experience with backend technologies like Node.js or PHP.
  • Problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
  • Stay updated with web trends and best practices.

Salary: £35,000 - £50,000 per year

Web Developer - Hybrid

Develop websites and applications while working in both remote and in-office settings. Balance technical skills with collaboration and adaptability.

Expectations:

  • Expertise in responsive design and cross-platform development.
  • Strong communication skills for team collaboration in hybrid environments.
  • Proficiency in frontend and backend technologies.
  • Ability to adapt to changing workflows and meet deadlines.

Salary: £40,000 - £55,000 per year

Back-End Web Developer

Build and maintain server-side components to ensure robust and scalable web applications. Collaborate with teams to integrate front-end and back-end seamlessly.

Expectations:

  • Proficiency in server-side languages like Python, PHP, or Node.js.
  • Experience with database systems like MySQL or MongoDB.
  • Knowledge of API integration and RESTful services.
  • Ability to debug and optimize back-end processes.
  • Strong collaboration skills with front-end developers and other stakeholders.

Salary: £45,000 - £60,000 per year
